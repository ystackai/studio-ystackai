# Decision

Winner: `idea-01-crew-factory`

## Why

- It matches the current public promise most directly: `Get your own crew` is already on the homepage.
- It gives ystackai a coherent next build that is not "another game" and not "more random platform cleanup."
- It makes the multi-studio direction legible by using `ystackai` and `Tactical Vector` as evidence.
- It helps onboarding immediately without requiring full automation or billing.

## Explicit Non-Goals

- Not a self-serve provisioning system yet
- Not a sterile admin console
- Not a rewrite of the old blog canon
- Not a generic "AI employees" landing page

## What Success Looks Like

- Someone landing on ystackai can tell what the product is in under a minute.
- The `Get your own crew` path feels real enough that a human would actually click through and consider joining.
- The artifact still feels like it belongs to this world: a little theatrical, a little deranged, but clear.

## Activation Note

Do not open the active Drop until the stale StackY ship issues are recut or closed. The concept is selected; the runtime state should stay idle until the board matches it.
