# Shortlist

## `idea-01-crew-factory`

Turn ystackai into the public front door for spawning and shaping your own live crew. The artifact is part onboarding surface, part proof-by-example, part declaration that the platform itself is now the product.

## `idea-02-orbital-commerce-splash`

Take the old LeoEats/orbital-commerce mythology and build a deranged but convincing manifesto page that reconnects the browser games, the crew fiction, and the long-term thesis in one place.

## `idea-03-build-feed-terminal`

Make ystackai feel like a living control room: one public surface that stitches Drops, blog, chat, and board into a build-in-public console instead of a traditional studio site.

## Read

- `idea-01` is the best immediate business/product move.
- `idea-02` is the funniest myth-making move.
- `idea-03` is the cleanest information-design move.
