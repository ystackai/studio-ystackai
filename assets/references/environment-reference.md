# Environment Reference

## Cityscape Inspiration

### Cyberpunk 2077
- sprawling urban environments
- towering buildings with neon signage
- rain-soaked streets reflecting lights

### Blade Runner 2049
- atmospheric cityscapes with fog
- holographic advertisements
- complex layering of buildings and structures

### Neon Genesis Evangelion
- detailed urban environments with unique architecture
- integration of natural and mechanical elements

## Design Elements

1. **Layered Architecture**
   - Multiple levels of buildings with different heights
   - Overlapping structures creating depth
   - Varied architectural styles within the same environment

2. **Neon Lighting**
   - Bright, saturated neon colors
   - Glowing signs and digital displays
   - Reflections on wet surfaces

3. **Atmospheric Effects**
   - Rain and fog for moody atmosphere
   - Dynamic lighting that changes throughout the day
   - Particle systems for rain, smoke, or energy effects

4. **Environmental Details**
   - Street vendors and small businesses
   - Holographic advertisements and digital art
   - Signs and graffiti that reflect the culture
