# Character Design Inspiration

## Key References

### Cyberpunk 2077
- Character designs by CD Projekt RED
- Focus on cybernetic implants and neon aesthetics
- Mix of organic and mechanical elements

### Blade Runner 2049
- Rain-soaked futuristic environments
- Complex character designs with detailed textures
- Neon lighting and atmospheric effects

### Neon Genesis Evangelion
- Mecha designs with organic elements
- Complex color palettes with neon accents
- Emotional character expressions

## Design Principles

1. **Fusion of Organic and Mechanical**
   - Combine natural textures with high-tech materials
   - Maintain human-like proportions while adding cybernetic elements

2. **Neon Aesthetics**
   - Use glowing highlights and neon colors
   - Create contrast between dark backgrounds and bright elements

3. **Functional Cybernetics**
   - Design implants that serve a purpose
   - Ensure cybernetic elements are believable and integrated

4. **Emotional Depth**
   - Character expressions should convey personality
   - Body language should reflect mood and state
