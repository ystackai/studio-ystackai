# Environment Concept: Neon District

## Overview

The Neon District is the primary setting for the game's early levels. This environment represents a sprawling cyberpunk city district filled with neon lights, rain-soaked streets, and towering holographic billboards.

## Design Elements

- **Visual Composition**:
  - Wide, sprawling cityscape with towering buildings
  - Rain-soaked streets reflecting neon lights
  - Holographic advertisements and digital displays
  - Fog and atmospheric effects
  - Glitch effects on digital surfaces

- **Color Scheme**:
  - Primary: Deep black (`#0A0A0A`) for night sky and shadows
  - Secondary: Neon blue (`#00F3FF`) for building lights and highlights
  - Accent: Electric purple (`#8A2BE2`) for advertisements
  - Additional: Neon pink (`#FF00FF`) for critical systems and alerts

- **Mood/Atmosphere**:
  - Dark and moody
  - Futuristic yet lived-in
  - Constantly illuminated by neon lights
  - Rain-soaked and atmospheric
