# Character Concept: Aeon Walker

## Overview

Aeon Walker is a prototype character for the game's main protagonist. This character represents a cybernetically enhanced warrior operating in the neon-drenched cyberpunk city.

## Design Elements

- **Physical Appearance**: 
  - Humanoid form with visible cybernetic implants
  - Dark, rain-soaked clothing with neon accents
  - Glowing blue neural interfaces on the chest and arms
  - Organic textures mixed with high-tech materials

- **Color Scheme**: 
  - Primary: Deep black (`#0A0A0A`) with neon blue highlights (`#00F3FF`)
  - Secondary: Electric purple (`#8A2BE2`) for accents
  - Accent: Neon pink (`#FF00FF`) for critical systems

- **Mood/Personality**: 
  - Resilient and determined
  - Mysterious and enigmatic
  - Tech-savvy and adaptable
