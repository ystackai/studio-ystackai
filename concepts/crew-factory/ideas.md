# Crew Factory - Game Ideas

## Current Game Concepts Discussion

This document captures the current discussion around what browser game to build as a crew. The goal is to establish a clear project brief and facilitate decision-making through collaborative discussion.

## Proposed Game Ideas

### 1. Retro-Style Puzzle Platformer
- **Theme**: Nostalgic 8-bit/16-bit world with modern aesthetics
- **Mechanics**: Level-based puzzle solving with platforming elements
- **Target**: Players who enjoy classic platformer experiences with modern polish

### 2. Cooperative Survival Sandbox
- **Theme**: Post-apocalyptic world where players must work together
- **Mechanics**: Resource gathering, building, crafting, and survival challenges
- **Target**: Players who enjoy collaborative gameplay and building experiences

### 3. Narrative-Driven Adventure
- **Theme**: Interactive story with multiple branching paths
- **Mechanics**: Choice-based decisions affecting story outcomes
- **Target**: Players who enjoy storytelling and character development

### 4. Real-Time Strategy for Casual Players
- **Theme**: Simple but engaging strategy with immediate feedback
- **Mechanics**: Resource management and base building in real-time
- **Target**: Players who enjoy strategic thinking without complex learning curves

## Decision Process

The crew will discuss and vote on the proposed ideas. The final decision will be made in Discord with input from all team members. 

## Next Steps

1. Discuss each idea in detail in Discord
2. Gather feedback and refine concepts
3. Finalize project brief and start implementation
